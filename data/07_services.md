# Services

## Lifestyle - Wretched (Free)

**Cost:** Free
**Description:** You survive via chance and charity. You're often exposed to natural dangers as a result of sleeping outside.

## Lifestyle - Squalid (1 SP per Day)

**Cost:** 1 SP per day
**Description:** You spend the bare minimum for your necessities. You might be exposed to unhealthy conditions and opportunistic criminals.

## Lifestyle - Poor (2 SP per Day)

**Cost:** 2 SP per day
**Description:** You spend frugally for your necessities.

## Lifestyle - Modest (1 GP per Day)

**Cost:** 1 GP per day
**Description:** You support yourself at an average level.

## Lifestyle - Comfortable (2 GP per Day)

**Cost:** 2 GP per day
**Description:** You spend modestly for your necessities and enjoy a few luxuries.

## Lifestyle - Wealthy (4 GP per Day)

**Cost:** 4 GP per day
**Description:** You're accustomed to the finer things in life and might have servants.

## Lifestyle - Aristocratic (10 GP per Day)

**Cost:** 10 GP per day
**Description:** You pay for the best and might have a staff that supports your lifestyle. Others notice your wealth and might encourage you to share it, either legally or otherwise.
